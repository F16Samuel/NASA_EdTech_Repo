Welcome to Plagiarism Detector

In today's digital age, ensuring the integrity of academic and professional assessments is more crucial than ever. Plagiarism Guardian is a cutting-edge tool designed to address this challenge for both EdTech platforms and recruitment agencies.

Our system provides a robust solution for detecting plagiarism in online code submissions, helping to maintain fairness and authenticity. By employing advanced rule-based detection methods, it identifies suspicious similarities, whether it's exact matches, variable renaming, or structural similarities.

With Plagiarism Guardian, educational institutions can ensure that students' grades reflect their true understanding, and recruitment agencies can make fair hiring decisions based on genuine skills. This tool is essential for upholding the credibility and integrity of online assessments and job evaluations.
